HIGHLIGHTS and BENEFITS

  • Each team member understands their role
  • Issues are quickly identified/corrected
  • Users quickly see the result and give feedback
  • Users refine requirements and can add new ones (but not in a sprint!)
  • No time wasted on reports and meetings
